## Overview

中国科大星云诗社从 2020 年 11 月 18 日开始筹备，于 2021 年 9 月 5 日正式成立，是校内唯一一家专门研究中国诗歌（包括古体诗和现代诗）的社团。

The USTC Xing Yun Poetry Club began preparations on November 18, 2020, and was officially established on September 5, 2021. It is the only club on campus devoted exclusively to the study of Chinese poetry — both classical and modern.
